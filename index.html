7 Wonders of the World

Explore the most remarkable creations of mankind

Chichén Itzá (Mexico)

Chichén Itzá is a complex of Mayan ruins on Mexico’s Yucatán Peninsula. A massive step pyramid known as El Castillo dominates the ancient city, which thrived from around 600 A.D.

Christ the Redeemer (Brazil)

Christ the Redeemer is an iconic statue of Jesus Christ in Rio de Janeiro, standing 30 meters tall and overlooking the city from Mount Corcovado.

The Colosseum (Italy)

The Colosseum in Rome is an ancient amphitheater that once held up to 80,000 spectators, famous for gladiator fights and Roman public events.

The Great Wall of China

Stretching over 13,000 miles, the Great Wall of China is a symbol of ancient defensive architecture and one of the most iconic structures in the world.

Machu Picchu (Peru)

Built in the 15th century, Machu Picchu is an Incan citadel set high in the Andes Mountains in Peru, known for its sophisticated dry-stone construction.

Petra (Jordan)

Petra is a historical and archaeological city in southern Jordan, famous for its rock-cut architecture and water conduit system. It was the capital of the Nabataean Kingdom.

Taj Mahal (India)

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum in Agra, built by Mughal emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal. It's a symbol of eternal love.
